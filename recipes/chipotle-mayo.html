Chipotle Mayo

Description

Smokey and Savoury, Chipotle mayo (or aioli, for those of you too pretentious for 'mayo') is a delicious condiment and dip with a multitude of uses.

Try it on burritos or sandwiches, as a marinade for chicken, or as a dip for french fries.

Ingredients

  • Mayonnaise
  • Sour Cream
  • Fresh Lime Juice
  • Chipotles in Adobo

Steps

  1. Place two parts mayo, one part sour cream, chipotles, and a generous squeeze of fresh lime juice in a tall jar
  2. Using an immersion blender, puree the mixture until homogenous
  3. Add salt or additional lime to taste
