Tzatziki

Description

A delicious, healthy yogurt and cucumber sauce. Great both in wraps and served as a dip with veggies or pita chips

Refreshing and savoury, Tzatziki is a surefire hit at any event you bring it to. Try it as an alternative to fattier dips like ranch!

Ingredients

  • Greek Yogurt
  • Cucumber
  • Lemon Juice
  • Garlic
  • Black Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • Fresh Dill

Steps

  1. Peel cucumber to remove skin. Grate the cucumber finely, then mix in a pinch a salt and place in a collander to draw out excess water
  2. While the salt is drawing the excess water from the cucumber, crush and finely dice garlic, and finely chop the fresh dill
  3. Squeeze water out of the grated cucumber, either through a cheesecloth or with clean hands
  4. Mix one part cucumber with one part greek yogurt
  5. Add garlic, dill, lemon juice, black pepper, and olive oil to cucumber and yogurt. Mix well
  6. Eat immediately, or place in fridge for a few hours to allow the flavors to mingle and develop
